Game Services

  • The Domiboard App offers Ludo and quiz games, each with three difficulty levels: easy, medium, and hard.

  • Games can involve 2 or 4 players, with winners receiving game coins as rewards.
  • Game coins won contribute to your position on the weekly leaderboard.

Leaderboard and Stages

  • The leaderboard comprises stages numbered 1 to 10.
  • Users' positions determine their status on the leaderboard stages:
  • Users in the top 25% are promoted.
  • Users ranked between 25% and 50% remain in their current stage.
  • Users above 50% are demoted.
